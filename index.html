About Me

Hi there, I'm Will. I'm a student web developer at Bellevue University, located in Bellevue, Nebraska. I love front-end and UI/UX design because every project is its own unique puzzle that needs a complex custom solution. Back-end coding is great too, creating your own servers, and managing to send information to customers and update databases is extremely rewarding and really drives home the idea that I've actually made something.

After I graduate, I hope to find a full-stack web developer position that allows me to progress and grow my proffesional skills, learn new and exciting techniques, and be a part of a purpose driven team and company. I love to work hard, and really put my creativity and ingenuity to the test.

I'm originally from Colorado, so it's pretty natural that I love sunshine, adventure sports, traveling, a great book, and technology.
